## 1. Vibe Coding (General)

**Why it's trending:** Andrej Karpathy's February 2025 tweet created an entire ecosystem. Collins Dictionary named "vibe coding" Word of the Year 2025. Google searches surged 2,400%. Product Hunt's trending categories for April 2026 list "Vibe Coding Tools" first. 40% of new SaaS MVPs are now built primarily with vibe coding.

**Who dominates:**
- Forbes (multiple dedicated articles from Jodie Cook, Bernard Marr — March/April 2026)
- Lovable (self-promotional but very high-authority guide page)
- NinjaTech.ai (12 Best Vibe Coding Tools in 2026 — comprehensive)
- Daily.dev (April 7, 2026 — high-authority developer publication)
- Medium (dozens of posts, some with 100+ claps)
- YouTube (high-view comparison videos)
- Reddit (r/vibecoding subreddit now active)

**Why it's hard to rank:** Extremely broad keyword. Every publication from Forbes to small dev blogs is covering this. The term itself is now a commodity. Specific sub-angles may still have gaps (see good-contenders.md).

**Who dominates:**

- Atomic Robot (Feb 2026 — "AI Writes Better Code, We're Getting Worse at Reviewing It")
